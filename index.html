In Memory of David Flowers (1963-2018)

We hold this event every year to remember the life of David Flowers. He was in no way a professional bike rider (in fact he often rode in his jean shorts!) but he enjoyed riding and connecting with his family and church members.

This year we will be raising money to start a scholarship in David's name.

Please join us for the bike ride if you are able! Feel free to bike, walk, or run. Check out the Registration page to register, pay, and reserve your shirt size.
